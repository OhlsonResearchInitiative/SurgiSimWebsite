OtoVIS is a high-fidelity 3D iOS and Android based ear simulator with specialized otology anatomy and pathology modules for medical education. Designed to meet MCCQE, CFPC, and CSOHNS learning objectives, it serves as a mobile resource for general practitioners to learn and review core otology topics, develop diagnostic skills, provide initial management, patient consent and determine when to refer to an Otolaryngologist.
