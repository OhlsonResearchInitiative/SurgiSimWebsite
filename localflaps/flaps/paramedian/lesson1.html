PARAMEDIAN FOREHEAD FLAP
LESSON 1/8
Description
  • Interpolated axial flap based on supratrochlear artery in the forehead
  • Commonly used to reconstruct distal nasal defects
  • Requires 2 stage reconstruction:
    • Stage 1: Elevation, thinning, and inset of flap
    • Stage 2: Division of pedicle approximately 10 - 14 days later
