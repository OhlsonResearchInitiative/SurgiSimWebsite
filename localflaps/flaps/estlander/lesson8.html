ESTLANDER
LESSON 8/8
Potential Complications
  • Creates fullness or blunting at oral commissure which can be apparent when mouth is open
  • Flap necrosis can result from division or kinking of the labial artery, or from an unusually large flap
  • Imprecise approximation of the vermillion results in visible notching of the lip
  • Skin hypesthesia is expected, this may lead to drooling or oral incompetence
