Z-PLASTY
LESSON 4/8
Pre-Operative Considerations
  • The base of each Z-plasty triangle should have high laxity to allow for proper pivoting
  • Avoid structures such as the eyelid, oral commissure, and ala to prevent distortion by scarring
  • The resulting central limb is reoriented such that it can be concealed within relaxed skin tension lines (RSTLs) or in a border between facial aesthetic regions
