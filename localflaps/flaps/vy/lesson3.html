V-Y ADVANCEMENT FLAP
LESSON 3/8
Key Measurements
  • Defect area = D*d
  • The length of the triangle (H) is typically 3 to 4 times the width of the surgical defect (h)
  • The height of the triangle (D) is usually 1.5 to 2 times the length of the original base or the “leading-edge” of the flap (d)
  • For different angles:
    • Ø = 60, H = D
    • Ø = 40, H = 1.4*D
    • Ø = 30, H = 1.9*D
