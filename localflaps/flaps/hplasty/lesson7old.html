LESSON 7/8
Post-Operative Considerations

    Immediate:

  • Initial flap sutures are removed at 5 days
  • No special dressings recommended
  • Consider using petroleum-based ointments twice daily along incision line. Avoid topical antibiotic ointments/creams (ex: Polysporin) as these can cause a significant erythema reaction

    Long-term:

  • Scar massage and application of silicone products once incisions have healed
  • Consider dermabrasion 6 weeks after procedure
  • Consider steroid injections for pin-cushioning or trap door deformities
