Privacy Policy

OtoVIS (iOS App)

At OtoVIS, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, sharing, and storage for the OtoVIS iOS application.

We do not collect any data of any kind from users of the OtoVIS app. Your usage of the app is entirely private, and no personal information, analytics, usage data, or any other type of data is collected by us.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share. We do not share any information with third parties, organizations, or individuals.

Data Storage

We do not store any data from our users. All interactions with the OtoVIS app remain solely on your device and are not transmitted to external servers or storage systems.
